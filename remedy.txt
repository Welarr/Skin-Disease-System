dis eczma
+Stop use of irritants such as Vaseline, mineral oils and soap
+Avoid extreme tempretures
+Avoid contact with wool
+Apply hydrocortisone cream until lesions clear
+Use antohistamines
ENDDISEASE

dis ringworm
+Apply Whitefields ointment twice a day for a minimum of 4 weeks
+ UseGriseofulvin 500mg once a day 2-6 weeks (adults); 10 -15mg once a day 2 – 6 weeks (children)
ENDDISEASE

dis athletesfoot
+Keep space in-between the toes dry eg dry skin thoroughly after washing, exposing to air, GV paint, cotton socks
+Use Imidazole/ Whitefields cream for 4 weeks
ENDDISEASE

dis thrush
+GV solution
+Potassium permanganate dressing
+Nystalin Ointment twice daily for 2 weeks
ENDDISEASE

dis chickenpox
+Calamine lotion or phenol-zinc lotion
+Antihistamine
+Use betadine scrub and chlorhexidine 1% mouthwash
ENDDISEASE

dis madurafoot
+Take patient to surgery to remove the small lesions
ENDDISEASE

dis impetigo
+Avoid sharing things such as clothes, towels, Vaseline
+Dress affected areas with GV, saline solution, betadine solution
+Don’t use Vaseline, use aqueous cream instead
+Doxacilin 250 mg / 4 times a day for 7-10 days
ENDDISEASE

dis erythrasma
+An imidazole cream twice daily for 4 weeks. 
If not effective or recurrent infection, use:
	-Erythromycin 250 mg 4 times daily for 2 weeks 
	-Erythromycin 2% lotion twice daily for 4 weeks
ENDDISEASE

dis yaws
+Benzathine penicillin 2,4 million units per IM injection, repeat after two weeks. For children benzathine penicillin 1,2 million units per IM injection, repeat after two weeks. 
+In case of penicillin-allergy: Erythromycin 500 mg 4 times daily for 2 weeks.
ENDDISEASE

dis leprosy
+Rifampicine 600 mg once a month under supervision plus dapsone (DDS) 100 mg daily for 6 months unsupervised.
ENDDISEASE

dis noma
+Observe oral hygiene
+Doses of penicillin
+High protein diet
ENDDISEASE
